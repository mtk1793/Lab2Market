## SLIDE 4 ‚Äî The Problem (Part 1): Supply Chain Difficulty

**Core Problem Statement:**
> Reliable spare parts supply is critical for remote industries ‚Äî but remote industries have difficult access to bulky metallic spare part inventory.

### Current Delivery Options & Their Limitations

| Method | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|
| **Helicopter (Chopper)** | Fast | Costly; Light loads only |
| **Supply Vessel** | Cheaper; Can carry heavy loads | Very slow |

Neither option is ideal ‚Äî there is no fast, affordable, high-capacity delivery mechanism for remote sites.

### Consequences of a Damaged Critical Part

When a critical metal component fails at a remote site, the downstream effects are severe:

1. **Process Shutdown** ‚Äî Operations halt entirely
2. **Costly Downtime** ‚Äî Every hour offline translates to significant revenue loss
3. **Safety Concerns** ‚Äî Equipment failures in offshore environments pose serious risks to personnel and infrastructure

## SLIDE 5 ‚Äî The Problem (Part 2): Scale of Inventory Costs

**Root Cause:**
E&P (Exploration & Production) companies are forced to maintain large, costly inventories of rarely used parts because of long supply lead times and the critical nature of certain components. This creates a massive financial burden.

### Industry Evidence

| Statistic | Source |

| **Up to $88 million** in annual downtime costs | GE Oil & Gas |

> These figures illustrate that the status quo ‚Äî physical inventory stocked "just in case" ‚Äî is both financially and operationally unsustainable for remote industries.

## SLIDE 6 ‚Äî The Solution: On-Demand Digital Inventory (Overview)

**The Shift:**
Move from **physical inventory** ‚Üí **digital inventory**

**The Principle:**
Print parts **on-site** and **just-on-time** instead of storing them **just-in-case**

This means remote facilities only need to store digital design files rather than thousands of physical metal components. When a part breaks, it is printed on-site using the stored digital blueprint ‚Äî dramatically reducing both inventory overhead and downtime.

**Summary:** Alma-Tech's just-on-time approach addresses every major weakness of the traditional OEM supply model, while also delivering environmental benefits through reduced transportation.
