# NRC IRAP Meeting Preparation — AddManuChain

**Purpose:** Present AddManuChain to NRC IRAP Industrial Technology Advisor (ITA)
**Website:** https://addmanuchain.vercel.app/
**Goal:** Secure ITA buy-in to advance toward a formal funding proposal

---

## MEETING OBJECTIVE

Walk the ITA through AddManuChain's:
1. Problem and market validation (customer discovery evidence)
2. Solution and website demo
3. Technical development roadmap (what IRAP funding would enable)
4. Ask: ITA advocacy + next steps toward a proposal

---

## WHAT TO PRESENT

### 1. The Problem (2 min)
- Industrial manufacturers in mining, marine, offshore energy, and defence face critical spare parts challenges: long lead times, obsolete parts, sole-source dependencies
- When a critical machine goes down, the inability to source a part quickly costs tens of thousands per day
- Current supply chains are fragile, globalized, and not designed for low-volume, high-criticality parts

### 2. The Solution — AddManuChain (2 min)
- A digital platform connecting industrial operators with certified additive manufacturing (3D printing) service providers
- Enables on-demand production of certified spare parts — locally, quickly, and traceably
- Digital thread: from part file → qualified manufacturer → certified output → delivery

**Live Demo of https://addmanuchain.vercel.app/**
- Walk through the core user flow
- Highlight key features: part search, supplier matching, traceability/certification layer
- Keep it to 3–4 minutes max

### 3. Market Validation (2 min)
- 70+ customer discovery interviews completed through Lab2Market program
- Key segments validated: mining maintenance, offshore marine, defence, aerospace
- Pain point confirmed across segments: parts obsolescence, lead time, supply chain fragility
- Early signals: operators willing to pilot, AM engineers interested in platform access

### 4. Technical Roadmap (2 min) — What IRAP Funds
IRAP funds **technical innovation and R&D**, not marketing. Frame the ask around:
- [ ] Secure digital part file management (encryption, IP protection, access control)
- [ ] Supplier qualification algorithm / matching engine
- [ ] Digital traceability layer (from CAD file to certified printed part)
- [ ] Integration with industry standards (e.g., AS9100, ISO 9001, ASTM AM standards)
- [ ] Pilot deployment with 1–2 industrial clients

### 5. The Ask (1 min)
> "We're looking for an ITA who sees the potential here to work with us on structuring a project proposal. We want to understand what IRAP can fund in our next phase and how to align our technical milestones with IRAP's requirements."
