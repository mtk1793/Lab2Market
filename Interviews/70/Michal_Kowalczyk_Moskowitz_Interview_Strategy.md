## RED FLAGS vs. GREEN FLAGS

**RED FLAGS:**
- "All our sites are already using [standard system/approach]" (no pain point)
- "I don't actually have authority to change site practices" (limited influence)
- "Maintenance is just a cost center, not a strategic lever" (wrong motivation)

**GREEN FLAGS:**
- Clear quantification: "We could save $XX million by standardizing approach"
- Acknowledged pain: "Knowledge doesn't transfer fast enough between sites"
- Data insights: Shows understanding of what data exists vs. what insights are extracted
- Authority: "I can drive adoption across Kinross"
- Champion potential: "Every mining company worldwide has this problem"
