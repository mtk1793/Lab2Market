# Interview Questions: Nathalie Sykora
**Position:** Chief Marine and Digital Solutions Officer, The CSL Group Inc.  
**Focus Areas:** Maritime supply chain, digital transformation, workforce management, international operations

---

## Core Questions (5)

1. **Global Supply Chain Coordination in Maritime**
   - In a globally distributed maritime operation like CSL's, how do teams currently coordinate information across vessels, ports, and different geographies?
   - What's the biggest challenge in keeping real-time visibility into material flows, inventory, and operational status across your fleet?

2. **Technology Integration & Organizational Silos**
   - When new digital solutions are introduced, what typically prevents different departments (operations, engineering, procurement, planning) from working seamlessly together?
   - How do you currently address the friction between legacy systems and newer digital tools that teams need to adopt?

3. **Decision-Making Speed in Complex Operations**
   - In the maritime sector, how much time does it typically take for operational data to translate into actual decisions that improve efficiency or safety?
   - What information gaps slow down decision-making today?

4. **Technology Adoption Barriers**
   - Looking across the maritime and shipping industries broadly, what's prevented widespread adoption of modern digital supply chain solutions?
   - What would a solution need to demonstrate before your organization would commit to major change and investment?

5. **Measuring Value & ROI**
   - What metrics or KPIs do you track to evaluate whether a digital initiative is working—beyond just cost savings?
   - How do you assess whether a new technology is solving a real problem or just adding complexity?

---

## Bonus Questions (2)

**Bonus 1:** If you could redesign one aspect of how your global supply chain currently operates—from data flow to decision-making to execution—what would it be and why?

**Bonus 2:** Thinking ahead 3–5 years, what's the one digital capability you think maritime companies absolutely need to build but most haven't yet?

## At End of Interview

*Based on what you've described, we're exploring whether a digital supply chain platform that connects real-time visibility with collaborative decision-making across departments could address these gaps. Does that direction resonate with the problems you see?*
