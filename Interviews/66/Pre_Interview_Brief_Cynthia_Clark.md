## RELEVANCE TO LAB2MARKET

Cynthia's expertise directly addresses Lab2Market's core challenges:

1. **Supplier Discovery & Network Building** — WISC work shows how supply chain leaders identify, vet, and connect with emerging talent and suppliers. Lab2Market needs to solve the same for distributed 3D printing facilities.

2. **Certification & Compliance** — As an industry advocate, she understands how certification systems, regulatory requirements, and quality assurance shape supplier credibility (relevant to Lab2Market's certification module).

3. **Capacity Utilization & B2B Relationships** — Her supply chain perspective spans workforce planning, resource optimization, and builder-distributor relationships across industries (automotive, energy, manufacturing).

4. **Government & Policy Alignment** — She bridges private sector and government. Understanding her approach to advocating for supply chain modernization could inform Lab2Market's go-to-market strategy with procurement departments.

5. **Emerging Supplier Ecosystems** — WISC's mission to develop next-generation supply chain leaders parallels Lab2Market's vision of creating distributed, certified local supplier networks.

---

## KEY INTERVIEW THEMES

1. **Distributed Supplier Networks:** How do supply chain leaders evaluate and trust distributed suppliers they've never worked with before?

2. **Certification as Currency:** What role does certification/compliance play in supplier selection and buyer confidence in distributed networks?

3. **Capacity Visibility:** How critical is real-time visibility into supplier capacity, and what systems would procurement managers want?

4. **Emerging Suppliers:** What enables established buyers to work with new/small suppliers? What barriers exist?

5. **Industry Alignment:** From her policy advocacy work, what does she see as the biggest friction points in Canada's supply chain that a platform could solve?

6. **Workforce & Skills:** How does the emergence of new manufacturing technologies (like 3D printing) create workforce and skills challenges that intermediaries need to address?
