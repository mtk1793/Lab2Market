## Interview Goals (Per Dr. Nasiri Context)

Based on Dr. Nasiri's background in **power systems, energy, and offshore renewable energy**, focus questions on:

1. **Offshore Wind/Energy Infrastructure:**
   - Spare parts challenges for offshore wind farms, tidal energy, remote substations
   - Predictive maintenance vs. reactive breakdown scenarios
   - Certification requirements for critical electrical/mechanical components

2. **Power Grid & Utility Operations:**
   - Transformer, switchgear, and substation component obsolescence
   - Emergency repair scenarios and downtime costs
   - Regulatory barriers to alternative manufacturing approaches

3. **Remote Operations:**
   - Parts availability for isolated installations
   - Supply chain constraints (Arctic, offshore platforms, remote grids)
   - Working capital tied up in "just-in-case" inventory

4. **AI & Digital Transformation:**
   - Current adoption of AI for predictive maintenance, inventory optimization
   - Appetite for platform-based solutions vs. in-house development
   - Data sharing concerns and IP protection requirements
