## üìö Deep Research Completed

### 1. **Demand-Driven MRP (DDMRP)**

**What is it:**
- Evolution of traditional MRP (Material Requirements Planning)
- **Buffer-driven** vs forecast-driven planning
- Strategic decoupling points in supply chain

**Buffer Zones:**
- üü¢ **Green Zone:** Trigger new orders (order generation)
- üü° **Yellow Zone:** Expected consumption during lead time (reorder point)
- üî¥ **Red Zone:** Safety buffer (shock absorber for variability)

**Key Parameters:**
- **Average Daily Usage (ADU):** Dynamically adjusted based on actual demand
- **Decoupled Lead Time:** Time to replenish buffer
- **Lead Time Factor:** Multiplier (longer lead time = smaller factor ‚Üí more frequent ordering)
- **Variability Factor:** Accounts for supply/demand uncertainty

**Application to AddManuChain:**
- Use yellow/red zones to trigger AM production
- Position as "demand-driven digital inventory"
- AI optimizes buffer levels (but solve fundamentals first)
- Integrate with MRP engine for stock-vs-print decisions

**Resources:**
- Demand Driven Institute (DDI)
- Book: "Demand Driven Material Requirements Planning" by Carol Ptak & Chad Smith
- SAP has DDMRP add-on for S/4HANA

### 2. **Working Capital Management (CFO Perspective)**

**Definition:**
- Current assets - current liabilities
- Inventory = major working capital component

**Why CFOs Care:**
- **Opportunity cost:** Money in warehouse can't be invested in growth
- **Cash flow:** High inventory ties up cash
- **Balance sheet:** Shows inefficiency to investors/board

**Industry Benchmarks:**
- Total inventory holding cost: **20-30% of inventory value per year**
- Breakdown:
  - Storage/warehouse: 6-8%
  - Insurance: 2-3%
  - Obsolescence: 8-12%
  - Cost of capital: 8-10%

**CFO Priorities:**
1. Free up working capital for growth investments
2. Improve cash conversion cycle
3. Reduce balance sheet inefficiency
4. Increase ROI on assets

**Application to AddManuChain:**
- **Lead with:** "Free up $X million in working capital"
- **Example:** $10M inventory @ 25% holding cost = $2.5M/year
  - Reduce inventory 30% ‚Üí Free up $3M capital + save $750K/year
- **Target audience:** CFO (not just supply chain manager)
- **Business case language:** Working capital liberation, cash flow improvement, ROI
