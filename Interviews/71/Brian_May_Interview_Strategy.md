> **Listen for:**
> - **A) Obsolescence:** legacy submarine/vessel components no longer commercially available
> - **B) Lead Times:** 12-24+ month waits for specialized military-grade parts

> - **D) Testing/Qualification Delays:** getting new parts certified for use in defence platforms
