# Interview Questions: Bob Hedrick
**Position:** President & Co-founder, CAMufacturing Solutions, Inc.  
**Focus Areas:** Additive manufacturing software (AddMan), CAD/CAM systems, manufacturing automation, product development

---

## Core Questions (5)

1. **Software & Manufacturing Ecosystem Evolution**
   - Across the manufacturing software industry, how have vendor relationships and product ecosystems evolved in the last decade?
   - What's the biggest challenge manufacturers face when adopting new software tools alongside their existing systems?

2. **The Research-to-Product Gap**
   - In your experience moving innovations from research labs into commercial products, what are the biggest reasons promising ideas never make it to market?
   - How do you decide which research outcomes have genuine market demand versus being interesting but niche?

3. **Vendor Integration and Interoperability**
   - When customers need to integrate multiple manufacturing software solutions (CAD/CAM, AM simulation, production planning), what problems typically arise?
   - How much integration friction exists in today's manufacturing software landscape?

4. **Scaling Across Market Segments**
   - What's the difference between selling manufacturing software to small machine shops versus enterprise manufacturers?
   - What makes adoption slower or faster in different customer segments?

5. **Education & Ecosystem Adoption**
   - How critical is training and ecosystem support to the success of new manufacturing technologies?
   - What barriers prevent the broader manufacturing industry from adopting cutting-edge tools more quickly?

---

## Bonus Questions (2)

**Bonus 1:** Over 13 years of product development, what patterns keep repeating in how the manufacturing software market adopts new innovations?

**Bonus 2:** If you could identify one critical gap in today's manufacturing software ecosystem that's preventing companies from working more efficiently, what would it be?
