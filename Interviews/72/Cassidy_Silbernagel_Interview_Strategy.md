## CORE INTERVIEW QUESTIONS (5 Main + 2 Side Questions)

### MAIN QUESTION 1: What Is the Biggest Barrier Stopping More Companies from Adopting AM?

> **"Cassidy, from your experience leading AM projects at Exergy with clients across industries — what is the single biggest reason companies that could benefit from additive manufacturing still aren't using it at scale? Is it cost, design knowledge, supply chain, or something else?"**

> **Listen for:**
> - **A) Design knowledge gap:** engineers don't know how to design for AM (DfAM)
> - **B) Cost/ROI uncertainty:** can't justify AM vs. conventional machining at current volumes
> - **C) Supply chain fragmentation:** finding the right printer, material, and post-processing in one place is hard
> - **D) Qualification/certification:** no clear path to prove a 3D-printed part meets spec
> - **E) Trust/risk aversion:** clients don't trust the technology for production-critical parts

> **Follow-up:** "When you've successfully converted a skeptical client to AM, what was the turning point that made them commit?"
