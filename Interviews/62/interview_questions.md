**Position:** Executive Advisor Innovation, Canadian Natural Resources Limited (CNRL)  
**Focus Areas:** Technology & innovation strategy, supply chain, energy sector transformation, sustainability & GHG

## Core Questions (5)

1. **Innovation Culture at Enterprise Scale**
   - In a company the size of CNRL, how do you actually move new ideas from concept to something the organization commits resources to?
   - What determines whether an innovation gets championed internally or gets killed by the existing business model?

3. **Speed & Risk in Large Organizations**
   - How does a Fortune 500 company like CNRL move fast enough to stay competitive when technologies are changing rapidly?
   - What's the difference between pilot-scale innovation and actually integrating something into operations?

4. **Cross-Functional Alignment & Execution**
   - When you're advising on innovation strategy at CNRL, what's the gap between engineering and commerce, or between what's technically possible and what's financially approved?
   - How do you build alignment across teams that move at different speeds?

5. **Supply Chain & Operational Visibility**
   - In energy operations specifically, what information gaps or coordination challenges create the most waste or risk in your workflows?
   - If your teams had perfect visibility and coordination across your supply chain, drilling operations, and logistics, what would actually change?
