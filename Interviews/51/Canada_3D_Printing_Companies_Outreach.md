### Outreach Strategy Recommendation
To maximize your customer discovery efforts for AddManuChain:
- **Marine/Offshore (1-11)**: Focus questions on the true costs of downtime, supply chain friction for remote sites, and regulatory hurdles in certifying non-OEM parts.
