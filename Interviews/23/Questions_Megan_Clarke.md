## Interview Questions

### Opening (2-3 minutes)

1. **Thank you for taking the time to speak with me! Can you tell me about your current role and background?**

2. **How did you become interested in this field?**

---

### Industry Context (5 minutes)

3. **Can you describe the industry you work in and the key challenges it faces?**
   - *Probe:* What are the biggest operational pain points?
   - *Probe:* How does spare parts management factor into your work?

4. **What does your current supply chain look like for parts and components?**
   - *Probe:* Any bottlenecks or challenges?
   - *Probe:* How do you handle urgent or unexpected part needs?

---

### Manufacturing & Technology (8 minutes)

5. **What manufacturing processes does your organization use currently?**
   - *Probe:* Any experience with additive manufacturing / 3D printing?
   - *Probe:* If yes, for what applications? If no, why not?

6. **How do you currently manage inventory for spare parts?**
   - *Probe:* Physical warehousing? Just-in-time? Digital systems?
   - *Probe:* What's the biggest challenge in inventory management?

7. **What role does certification play in your industry?**
   - *Probe:* What certifications are required for parts?
   - *Probe:* Is certification a barrier to adopting new manufacturing approaches?

8. **Are you using any AI or digital tools for inventory management or maintenance planning?**
   - *Probe:* What works well? What's missing?
   - *Probe:* Interest in AI-driven inventory optimization?

---

### Digital Inventory Concept (5 minutes)

9. **I'm researching a concept called "digital inventory"—storing part designs digitally and printing on-demand rather than warehousing physical parts. Does that concept resonate with your experience?**
   - *Probe:* What would make it valuable?
   - *Probe:* What concerns or barriers would you have?

10. **If you could solve one spare parts or manufacturing challenge, what would it be?**

11. **What would convince your organization to adopt a new approach like digital inventory?**
    - *Probe:* ROI requirements? Proof points? Pilot programs?

---

### Ecosystem & Network (3 minutes)

12. **What's the manufacturing and technology ecosystem like in Nova Scotia?**
    - *Probe:* Any gaps in local capabilities?
    - *Probe:* Who are the key players?

13. **Is there anyone else you'd recommend I speak with for my research?**

---

### Closing (2 minutes)

14. **What advice would you give to someone building technology for this industry?**

15. **Would you be open to a follow-up conversation or feedback on prototypes as my research progresses?**

16. **Thank you so much for your time—this has been incredibly valuable!**
