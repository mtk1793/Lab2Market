## DURING THE MEETING

### Structure (Recommended Order)

**Phase 1: Validate Segment (Q1-Q3, 20 min)**
- Morning Worry: What actually worries him?
- Metric Question: What makes him look good to his boss?
- Catastrophic Failure: What scenario keeps him up at night?

→ **Listen for:** Which pain point dominates his thinking?

**Phase 2: Horizontal Segmentation (Q4-Q5, 15 min)**
- Two solution options; no "better" answer
- Trade-off question forcing real prioritization

→ **Listen for:** Which option makes him say "YES, that's exactly what I need"?

**Phase 3: Reject Platonic Solution (Q6-Q7, 10 min)**
- Would he want everything integrated?
- Which MVP would he fund first?

→ **Listen for:** Does he prefer focused solution or integrated platform?

**Phase 4: Deep Dive His Segment (Q8-Q10, 15 min)**
- Ask detailed follow-ups based on which cluster he falls into

→ **Listen for:** How acute is the pain? What's he willing to pay?

**Phase 5: Happiness & Validation (Q11, 5 min)**
- If we solve his primary pain, would he be "deliriously happy"?
- Would he champion this to peers?

→ **Listen for:** Champion potential; quantifiable value.

**Phase 6: Go-to-Market & Ecosystem (Q12-Q13, 10 min)**
- How would he discover solutions like this?
- Thales/Cove relationship clarity

→ **Listen for:** Channel insights; competitor/partner dynamics.
