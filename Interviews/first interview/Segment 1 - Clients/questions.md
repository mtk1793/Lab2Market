### 6. What outcomes matter most to you when sourcing these parts?

**Response:**
> "Lead time is a big deal. If we can get the part faster, that would be great."

**Critical Success Factors:**
1. **Lead time reduction** (Primary priority)
2. Quality and reliability (Implied from Q1 supplier choice)
3. Cost competitiveness (Secondary to speed)

**Key Insights:**
- Time-to-delivery is the #1 pain point
- Current lead times are unacceptable for business needs
- Faster delivery would provide significant competitive advantage
- Suggests project timelines are being negatively impacted by sourcing delays

**Strategic Implications:**
- Speed premium exists in market (willing to pay more for faster delivery)
- On-demand or rapid manufacturing capabilities highly valuable
- Local/regional sourcing preferred if lead times competitive
- Predictability in delivery timelines may be as important as absolute speed
