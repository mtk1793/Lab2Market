## KEY OUTREACH PRINCIPLES (For All Segments)

‚úÖ **DO:**
- Personalize every message (mention their specific work)
- Keep under 100 words
- Emphasize LEARNING, not selling
- Offer 15-20 minute timeframe
- Give easy out ("If timing isn't right, no worries")
